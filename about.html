Who we are?

We are a group of students from Batna University 2. We designed and programmed this website for displaying books. We hope you like it.

What?

Discover a world of knowledge at our book-centric hub. Explore diverse genres, find literary treasures, and delve into captivating narratives. From timeless classics to contemporary bestsellers, our platform offers a rich collection catering to every reader's passion and interest

How?

Combining HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, I crafted a dynamic book-centric website from scratch. HTML provided the structure, CSS added style and visual appeal, while JavaScript enhanced interactivity. PHP empowered functionality and server-side operations, seamlessly integrating with MySQL for robust data storage and retrieval. Through this holistic blend of languages and technologies, the website emerged as a user-friendly platform, offering an immersive experience for book enthusiasts to explore, engage, and discover literary treasures.
